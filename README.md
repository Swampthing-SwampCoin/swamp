# 🛡️ Swamp Coin (SWAMP)

**Currency for the Wild | Restoration through Blockchain**

Swamp Coin was created to restore balance to global ecosystems that reptiles and amphibians call home. As a future 501(c)(3) non-profit charitable organization, Swamp Coin leverages blockchain technology and Masternode infrastructure to fund the preservation of critical habitats and support animal rescues.

---

## 🌿 Our Mission

Ecosystem homeostasis is under constant threat. Swamp Coin provides a sustainable funding model for:
* **Habitat Restoration:** Funding projects that preserve and rebuild wetlands and swamplands.
* **Animal Rescues:** Supporting medical care and adoption fees for swamp-based species.
* **Community Governance:** Empowering a global network to decide the future of conservation funding.

---

## ⚡ Core Features

SWAMP is a finite, predictable, and egalitarian Proof-of-Work (PoW) cryptocurrency designed to outperform traditional fiat systems:

* **Masternode Network:** Supports network consensus and provides advanced services (InstantSend/PrivateSend).
* **ASIC Resistance:** YesPoWer hashing algorithm ensures CPU-only mining, maximizing decentralization.
* **Financial Sovereignty:** Permissionless, borderless, and transparent transactions without middle-men.
* **Deflationary Nature:** A fixed maximum supply of 40,000,000 SWAMP protects against fiat inflation.
